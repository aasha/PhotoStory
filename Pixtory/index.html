WHAT'S A PIXTORY?

Pixtory was born out of the philosophy that "All great photographs have a story behind them and the Story itself is as important as the photograph".

The 'Story' - whether it's the reason, an emotion, the inspiration, some facts about or the thought behind the photograph, plays an important role in defining the character of any picture.

Pixtory = Picture + Story
